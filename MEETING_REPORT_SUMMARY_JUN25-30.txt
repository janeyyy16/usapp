  values" button if typed values don't match.

  message. Fails open on network errors.

SP schedule date sync + timezone fix
- Bug: ticket 015789584139 was scheduled 7/2 in SP but showed 6/30 on
  our Work Map. Two root causes:
  1. `formatServicePowerDate` ran UTC timestamps through `new Date()`
     and read local-tz date → day-off-by-one. Rewrote to read y/m/d
     fragment directly from the string (handles ISO with timezone,
     compact YYYYMMDD, US MM/DD/YY).
  2. Sync was deliberately skipping `schedule_date` (old "CSR owns
     the date" rule). Reversed: SP wins on every sync; existing date
     kept only when SP sends nothing.
- New `parseScheduleDateForSql()` normalises to YYYY-MM-DD Postgres
  format or null.
- Existing-row lookup now fetches `schedule_date` too for comparison.
